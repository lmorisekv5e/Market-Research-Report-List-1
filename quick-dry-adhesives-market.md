<p><p>The quick dry adhesives market includes primarily two types: cyanoacrylate and epoxy-based adhesives. Cyanoacrylate adhesives, also known as superglues, are known for their fast bonding properties and are ideal for small repairs and bonding of various materials like plastics, metals, and ceramics. Epoxy-based adhesives offer stronger, more durable bonds and are often used in applications requiring resistance to heat and chemicals. Both types cater to diverse industrial and consumer needs, enhancing efficiency and performance in bonding tasks.</p></p>
