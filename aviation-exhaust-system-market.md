<p><ul><li>Engine Exhaust System</li><li>APU Exhaust System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aviation exhaust system market comprises two primary types: engine exhaust systems and auxiliary power unit (APU) exhaust systems. Engine exhaust systems are designed to manage and expel exhaust gases produced during aircraft engine operation, ensuring optimal performance and emissions control. APU exhaust systems, on the other hand, manage the byproducts of auxiliary power units, which provide electrical power and air conditioning while the aircraft is on the ground. Both systems are critical for safety, efficiency, and regulatory compliance in aviation.</p></p>
