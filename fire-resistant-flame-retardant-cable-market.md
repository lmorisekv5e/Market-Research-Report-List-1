<p><p>The Fire Resistant Flame Retardant Cable market is categorized into two main types: halogen-containing cables and halogen-free cables. Halogen-containing cables are designed to resist flames while releasing halogen gases when exposed to high temperatures, which can be toxic during a fire. In contrast, halogen-free cables are constructed without these compounds, making them safer in terms of smoke and toxicity, and are ideal for environments where safety is a priority. Both types are essential for enhancing fire safety in various applications.</p></p>
