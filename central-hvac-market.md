<p><p>The Central HVAC market primarily features two types: Split System Central HVAC and Packaged Unit Central HVAC. A Split System Central HVAC consists of separate indoor and outdoor units, allowing for greater flexibility and efficiency, making it suitable for larger spaces. Conversely, a Packaged Unit Central HVAC integrates all components into a single unit, typically installed on rooftops or outdoors, making it ideal for smaller buildings and installations where space is limited. Both systems provide effective climate control for various applications.</p></p>
