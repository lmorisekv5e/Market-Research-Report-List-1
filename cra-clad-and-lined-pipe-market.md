<p><p>The CRA (Corrosion Resistant Alloy) Clad and Lined Pipe market involves two primary types: CRA Clad Pipe and CRA Lined Pipe. CRA Clad Pipe features a thin layer of corrosion-resistant alloy bonded to a carbon steel substrate, providing enhanced durability in harsh environments. In contrast, CRA Lined Pipe consists of a pipe lined with a seamless layer of corrosion-resistant material, ensuring protection against corrosive media. Both types are essential for industries such as oil and gas, chemical processing, and power generation, offering increased lifespan and reliability.</p></p>
