<p><p>The plastic bucket market comprises various types based on material composition, each serving distinct purposes. HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) buckets are known for durability and chemical resistance, making them ideal for industrial use. PP (Polypropylene) buckets offer lightweight and flexibility, suitable for food storage. Shrink film buckets provide tamper-proof packaging. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) buckets are versatile but less commonly used for food due to safety concerns. LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) buckets are flexible, often used for household applications, with good impact resistance.</p></p>
