<p><strong>The Marine 4 Stroke Engine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Heavy Fuel Oil</li><li>Marine Diesel Oil</li><li>Marine Gas Oil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Marine 4 Stroke Engine market encompasses various fuel types, primarily Heavy Fuel Oil (HFO), Marine Diesel Oil (MDO), and Marine Gas Oil (MGO). HFO is a low-cost, high-viscosity fuel suitable for larger vessels, offering efficiency for long voyages. MDO, a blend of distillate and residual oils, provides better combustion and is used in medium-speed engines. MGO is a lighter, low-sulfur fuel designed to meet stringent emission regulations, ensuring cleaner operations in sensitive marine environments.</p></p>
