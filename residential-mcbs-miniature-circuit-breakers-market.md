<p><p>Residential Miniature Circuit Breakers (MCBs) are protective devices designed to automatically switch off electrical circuits in the event of overloads or short circuits. The market for MCBs is categorized into various types based on the number of poles: 1-pole MCBs are used for single-phase circuits; 2-pole units serve two-phase systems; 3-pole MCBs cater to three-phase systems, typically for larger loads; while 4-pole MCBs combine three phases and a neutral, offering comprehensive protection for complex residential electrical setups.</p></p>
