<p><p>The aircraft elevator market features various types, including horizontal stabilator elevators, which integrate both the stabilizer and elevator functions for improved aerodynamic efficiency. Elevons serve as control surfaces on delta wing aircraft, combining the functionalities of elevators and ailerons for enhanced maneuverability. Levcons are hybrid surfaces that act as both control surfaces and effective aerodynamic devices, often used in conjunction with other systems to optimize performance. Each type offers unique advantages in aircraft design and handling characteristics.</p></p>
