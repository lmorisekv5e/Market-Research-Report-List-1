<p><p>The Power Pre Stretched Film market is divided into two primary types: Coreless Power Pre Stretched Film and Cored Power Pre Stretched Film. Coreless films are designed for use without a cardboard core, which reduces waste and enhances portability, making them ideal for smaller packaging operations. In contrast, Cored Power Pre Stretched Films are wound on a cardboard core, providing structural support and compatibility with standard packaging equipment. Both types offer benefits in efficiency, cost-effectiveness, and material conservation, catering to various industrial needs.</p></p>
